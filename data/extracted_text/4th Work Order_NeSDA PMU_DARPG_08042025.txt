Sir,
I am directed to refer to QCI's work proposal submitted on 11th March, 2025 for the
extension of the QCI PMU set up for implementation of the recommendations of NeSDA 2021,
for another year i.e. from 01.04.2025 to 31.03.2026. In this regard, the undersigned is directed
to convey herewith the approval of the Competent Authority for extension of the PMU for
another year from 01.04.2025 to 31.03.2026. The terms and conditions of the PMU during the
extended period would be as under:
I.
Scope of Work:
⚫ Generation of NeSDA Way Forward monthly reports, with special emphasis on
strengthening of unified e-service delivery portals, conversion of offline services to e-
services, faceless aspect of mandatory e-services and suo-moto e-service delivery
mechanism for scalability & replicability
Monitoring and updation of NeSDA Way Forward Dashboard, with a focus on
leveraging uploaded inputs from States/UTS for reporting and insights
• Identification of bottlenecks in the implementation of recommendations and
dissemination of best practices
.
Conducting meetings and brainstorming sessions with State Governments, RTS Chief
Commissioners/Appellate Officers, etc., to improve e-service delivery
⚫ Facilitate DARPG to conduct National e-Governance Webinars (NeGW)
• Handholding and query resolution for all key stakeholders, i.e., SPOCS, RTS Chief
Commissioners/Appellate Officers, NIC, session speakers, etc
The proposed duration of the Project is from 01.04.2025 to 31.03.2026. The complete scope,
along with milestones, may be finalized by mutual agreement between the DARPG and QCI.
8/04/2025
II.
Composition, Manpower and financial Plan
The PMU team will comprise 3 QCI professionals as per the previously issued work
orders. The team will be working with DARPG from 1st April, 2025. The team will be working
with DARPG for a period of 1 year. The Department will assign a Single Point of Contact
(SPOC) for smooth coordination on project requirements. The professionals will have the
relevant qualifications and professional background required to fulfil the tasks outlined in the
previous section.
These professionals shall have the following skills and credentials:
⚫ The professionals might have been a part of any earlier PMU's set up at various
Ministries/ Departments by QCI
.
To help with specific analysis, at least 1 professional should have core expertise in
data analytics and must have graduated from Tier 1 college from India/ abroad
⚫ Apart from the above-mentioned points, DARPG may ask for professionals with
certain skills they deem necessary. This intimation should be done before signing of
the work order by both the parties
