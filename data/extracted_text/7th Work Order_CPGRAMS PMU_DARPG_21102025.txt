Sir,
In continuation of this Department's letter of even number dated 13-05-2025,
I am directed to refer to the proposal received from Quality Council of India (QCI)
on the above subject and to convey the approval of the competent authority for
extending the Work Order for setting up of Project Management Unit (PMU) of QCI
(with three members) at a cost of Rs.30,24,000/- (excluding taxes), for a period of
6 months (from 01-10-2025 to 31-03-2026) or till the implementation of NextGen
CPGRAMS whichever is earlier subject to the terms and conditions (Deliverables
and detailed plan of action) as specified below:-.
Deliverables
i. Review and streamlining existing categorization /taxonomy and mapping across
those Central Ministries/Departments that have volunteered by end of
November, 2025.
ii. Target of review of root cause analysis as a time bound process of 4 month,
including a review of the grievances/ disposal / feedback etc, covering about 85
Ministries/Departments
iii Study of Public Grievances System for select Ministries/Departments and
States/UTs to understand the flow of grievances and bottlenecks as a time bound
process of 4 months
iv. To prepare monthly reports on public grievances received on CPGRAMS on
the basis of multidimensional analysis with particular reference to performance of
Central Ministries and State Governments/ UTs by the 5th of each month with the
last acceptable date of 10 th of each month.
v. To publish Annual Report on CPGRAMS every year by 2nd/3rd Week of
December to enable the release during Good Governance Week Celebrations.
