I am directed to refer to this Department's letter of even number dated 07th
February, 2023 on the subject mentioned above and to convey the approval of the
Competent Authority for extending the Work Order for setting up of the Project

•
Progressive increase in number of mandatory services in States/UTS/ Identified
Ministries
• Saturation of e-Services in States/UTS/ Identified Ministries
•
•
Adoption of Unified Portal with special focus on North Eastern States for
creating more services on Unified Portal by States/ UTs/ Identified Ministries
Improving State/ UT/ Identified Ministry Portal parameters in line with NeSDA
2023 by strengthening all seven focus parameters with special emphasis on
Open Government Data (OGD), E-Participation and Leveraging Emerging
Technologies.
Integration of Service Plus, MyScheme and other such Portals of Meity with
States/UTS/ Identified Ministries portals for further strengthening them.
• To promote Delivery of faceless and suo-moto services by States/ UTS/
Identified Ministries
•
Improving of Identified Ministry Portals
•
•
Developing and Maintaining NeSDA Way Forward Portal to incorporate the
above parameters.
Any other related work assigned by the Department beyond the above
parameters from time to time.
