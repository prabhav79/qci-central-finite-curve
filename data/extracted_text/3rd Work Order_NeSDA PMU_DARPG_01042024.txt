Sir,
I am directed to refer to QCI's work proposal submitted in the month of April, 2024 for
the extension of the QCI PMU set up for implementation of the recommendations of NeSDA
2021, for another year i.e. from 01.04.2024 to 31.03.2025. In this regard, the undersigned is
directed to convey herewith the approval of the Competent Authority for extension of the PMU
for another year from 01.04.2024 to 31.03.2025. The terms and conditions of the PMU during
the extended period would be as under:
I.
Scope of Work:
Generation of NeSDA Way Forward monthly reports, with special emphasis on
strengthening of unified e-service delivery portals, conversion of offline services to eservices,
faceless aspect of mandatory e-services and suo-moto e-service delivery mechanism for
scalability & replicability
Monitoring and updating of NeSDA Way Forward Dashboard and analysis of the
uploaded inputs
Identification of bottlenecks in the implementation of recommendations and
dissemination of best practices
Conducting meetings and brainstorming sessions with State Governments, RTS Chief
Commissioners / Appellate Officers, etc., to improve e-service delivery
Facilitate DARPG to conduct National e-Governance Webinars (NeGW)
Stanya
Handholding and query resolution for all key stakeholders, i.e., SPOCs, RTS Chief
Commissioners / Appellate Officers, NIC, session speakers, etc.
Any other work assigned by the DARPG
The proposed duration of the Project is from 01.04.2024 to 31.03.2025. The complete scope,
along with milestones, may be finalized by mutual agreement between the DARPG and QCI.
II.
Composition, Manpower and financials

orders. The team will be working with DARPG from 1st April, 2024. The team will be working
with DARPG for a period of 1 year. The Department will assign a Single Point of Contact
(SPOC) for smooth coordination on project requirements. The professionals will have the
relevant qualifications and professional background required to fulfil the tasks outlined in the
previous section.
